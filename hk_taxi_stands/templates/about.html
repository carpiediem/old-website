Red Urban Taxis

Red taxis are permitted to go almost everywhere in the Hong Kong SAR, but they charge the highest rates to passengers. The only place a red taxi won't be able to take you is the south side of Lantau Island (you'll need a blue taxi for that).

Base Price $22.00 for first 2km

Green New Territories Taxis

Green taxis are permitted to go anywhere in six districts that make up most of the New Territories: Tuen Mun, Yuen Long, Tai Po, North, Ma On Shan, & Sai Kung (except Tseung Kwan O). They are also permitted to travel to and from ten locations outside that area.

  • Hong Kong International Airport
  • Shenzhen Bay Border Crossing
  • Tsing Yi Airport Express
  • Tsuen Wan MTR Station
  • Hang Hau MTR Station
  • Prince of Wales Hospital
  • Tseung Kwan O Hospital
  • Shun Lee Estate in Kwun Tong
  • Sha Tin Racecourse
  • Hong Kong Disneyland
Base Price $18.50 for first 2km
Normal Rate $1.40 per 200 meters travelled
per minute wait time
Discount Rate $1.00 per 200 meters travelled
per minute wait time
Switch to Discount Rate $60.50 ~32km
Blue Lantau Taxis

Blue taxis are permitted only on Lantau Island and Chek Lap Kok (the island that Hong Kong International Airport is on).

Base Price $17.00 for first 2km
Normal Rate $1.40 per 200 meters travelled
per minute wait time
Discount Rate $1.20 per 200 meters travelled
per minute wait time
Switch to Discount Rate $143.00 ~92km

To reach the discount rate in a blue taxi, you'd need to ride from the airport to Disneyland, then Tai O, Mui Wo and back to the airport.

Cross-Harbour Taxi Stands

If you flag a taxi off the street or at a normal taxi stand and ask it to take you to a destination on the other side of Victoria Harbour, your fare will not only include any fees paid for tunnels and bridges you pass through, you'll also pay a $10-15 return trip fee (for the driver to get back to his home territory). If, instead, you go to a cross-harbour taxi stand, you only need to pay the tunnel fee once. Typically, the driver will ask you which tunnel you'd like to use, since they have different prices and traffic patterns.
